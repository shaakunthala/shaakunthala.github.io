Short Bio | Shaakunthala

Hi, my name is Sameera Shaakunthala. (You can also call me Shaaku 😊)
I’m a Sri Lankan information technology professional.
Currently I live and work in the Netherlands.

I grew up in Ambalangoda - a coastal city in Sri Lanka.
I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Information and Communication Technology, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Information Security from University of Colombo School of Computing.

I work in the capacity of a JIRA Specialist/ Business Analyst in a globally renowned organization relating to automobile industry.
I built an exciting career from nothing, to the level of a subject matter expert of JIRA.
I have previously worked with teams in Sri Lanka, United States and India.
I have 8+ years of total work experience, most relating to Atlassian JIRA.

I am a Toastmaster, and the current Vice President Public Relations of HERE Netherlands Toastmasters Club.
My hobbies are watching science experiments on YouTube, reading, listening to music and motorcycling.
I have a passion for problem solving, debugging tech stuff as well as mentoring people while self-learning.

FAQ
